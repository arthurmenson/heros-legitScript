GoGoMeds is a state-licensed, LegitScript-certified online pharmacy that complies with all applicable federal and state regulations.

How Prescriptions Are Filled

  1. A licensed Heros Health provider reviews your health information and, when appropriate, issues a prescription.
  2. Prescriptions are securely transmitted to GoGoMeds for dispensing.
  3. Medications are shipped directly to the patient from GoGoMeds’ licensed facility.

Compounded Medications

When clinically appropriate, GoGoMeds prepares custom compounded medications through its licensed pharmacy facilities.

Compounded medications are prepared by state-licensed pharmacies when clinically appropriate. Compounded drugs are not FDA-approved, and the FDA does not verify their safety, effectiveness, or quality.

Heros does not prescribe FDA‑approved medications. Where FDA‑approved alternatives exist, your provider may discuss those options with you; compounded therapies may be used when clinically appropriate.
